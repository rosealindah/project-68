Art by 5th graders
Beautiful Art

This beautiful art work is created by fifth graders at Jhon James Audubon Elementry school it is a Mexican sugar skull created to celebrate "The Day of the Dead". On the day of the dead children like to create sugar skulls which are made up of sugar and decorated with edible decorations such as, icing or frosting, sprinkles, edible glitter and fondant. Here instead of making a edible sugar skull the fifth grade students have made paper skulls and insteead of using edible decorations they have used markers, gems, beads, and paper!
